📁 Raw Email Files (Recommended)
Upload a ZIP file containing folders of raw email files. This uses your complete analysis pipeline for better accuracy.

Folder Structure Requirements:

  • Method 1: Create folders named "phishing" and "legitimate"
  • Method 2: Create folders named "spam" and "ham"
  • Method 3: Use filenames containing "phishing/spam" or "legitimate/ham"
Example folder structure:
📦 training_data.zip
├── 📁 phishing/
│ ├── 📄 email1.txt
│ ├── 📄 email2.eml
│ └── ...
└── 📁 legitimate/
    ├── 📄 email1.txt
    ├── 📄 email2.eml
    └── ...
Accepts ZIP files (raw emails) or CSV files (structured data)

📊 Alternative: CSV Format

If you prefer to use structured data, upload a CSV with columns:

  • subject - Email subject line
  • body - Email body content
  • label - 0 for legitimate, 1 for phishing

💡 Training Tips:

  • Use at least 100 examples of each class (phishing and legitimate)
  • More diverse examples lead to better accuracy
  • Raw email files (.eml, .txt, .msg) work best
  • Training may take several minutes depending on data size
  • The model will automatically save after successful training
